What is The Stoa of AI?

The Stoa of AI is a membership community focused on AI, led by three founders (Zeneca, A Fox, and Frank Zuuring), offering research, education, community, and alpha at the frontier of artificial intelligence.

What does "Founding Member" mean?

Founding Members are the first to join The Stoa of AI. They lock in the introductory price of $9/month (or $90/year) forever — the price will increase for future members. Annual founding members also get a Founding Member badge and special recognition within the community.

Will the price really go up?

Yes. $9/month (or $90/year) is the founding member price. Once the founding period ends, the price increases. Founding members keep their rate locked in permanently.

Do I need technical AI knowledge to join?

Not at all. Whether you're a complete beginner, someone already experimenting with AI tools, or a technical expert — The Stoa of AI is designed to meet you where you are and help you level up.

Who are the founders?

The Stoa of AI is led by three founders. Zeneca is one of the most respected voices in the crypto/Web3 space, known for building large communities and identifying trends early. A Fox is a Web3 educator and cofounder of TokenPage, with a newsletter reaching over 22,000 subscribers. Frank Zuuring runs AMTEX Digital, bringing 30+ years of tech experience and deep expertise in AI agent implementation. Together, they bring a unique blend of community-building and technical expertise.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your membership at any time. However, if you cancel your founding membership, the locked-in rate is not guaranteed upon rejoining.
